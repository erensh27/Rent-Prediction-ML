Rent Trend Graphs

Exploratory visualizations of the training dataset

Rent vs Size

Scatter plot of monthly rent against property size in square feet. Highlights the positive but noisy relationship — larger properties tend to cost more, but city and locality drive significant variance.

Average Rent by City

Mean monthly rent grouped by city, sorted ascending. Mumbai is a clear outlier on the high end; Kolkata and Hyderabad sit on the affordable end of the distribution.

Feature Correlation

Pairwise Pearson correlations between numeric features and rent. Size and BHK have the strongest direct correlation with rent; bathrooms covary with both.

Feature Importances

Permutation importance scores from the trained pipeline on the held-out test set. Larger bars mean shuffling that feature degrades predictions more — i.e. the model relies on it more heavily.
